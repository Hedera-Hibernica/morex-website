Your account

Managing your account

Your dashboard shows your Morex Credits balance, recent transactions, and your business profile.

  • Balance — your current Morex Credits position.
  • Transaction history — a full record of all payments, downloadable for your accounts.
  • Profile — update your business details, what you offer, and contact information.
Payments

Making and receiving payments

  • Internet banking — log in and send a payment directly to another member.
  • Mobile app — use the Cyclos app to make or receive payments on the go.
  • QR code — display a QR code at your till for customers to scan and pay instantly.
Marketplace

Finding businesses to trade with

  • Directory — browse all member businesses by category or search by name.
  • Listings — members can post products and services for sale.
Credit

Understanding credit limits

  • You can spend up to your credit limit even if your balance is zero.
  • Bring your balance back toward zero by selling to other members.
  • Credit limits may be reviewed based on your trading activity.
Remember: Morex Credits cannot be redeemed for sterling. They can only be spent within Morex Network.
