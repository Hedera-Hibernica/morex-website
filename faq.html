Frequently asked questions

About the network
Morex Network is a business-to-business mutual credit network for SMEs across Moray. It allows member businesses to buy and sell with each other using interest-free trade credit — Morex Credits — rather than relying entirely on cash.
Morex Network is open to legitimate businesses and organisations based in or trading in Moray. You must be authorised to act on behalf of your business and provide accurate information when registering.
Details of any membership fees will be communicated clearly before you join. Any fees introduced will always be notified to members in advance.
Unlike a loyalty scheme, Morex Credits are a genuine trading currency used between businesses. Unlike traditional barter, you do not need a direct swap partner. You sell to any member, earn Morex Credits, then spend them with any other member.
How it works
Mutual credit is a system where businesses create credit together as they trade. When you sell, your Morex Credits balance rises. When you buy, it falls. The network always balances to zero. Visit our What is Morex Network? page for a full explanation.
